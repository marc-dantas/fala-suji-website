Fala Suji - The sweet language

What is Fala Suji?

Fala Suji is a conlang that aims to improve communication with fewer words than common languages. It's based on Toki Pona, by Sonja Lang

How to speak complex words with <200 word vocabulary?

There's a type of word called modifier. These words work as adjectives or adverbs to modify the sense of words.

To say a more complex word. You need to "disassemble" the word to translate it.

Learning the language

In this website, there's a complete documentation of Fala Suji, so you can read it and join our Discord community and learn much more!

Contribute!

On Fala Suji's Discord server, you can contribute to the language making suggestions to improve it and teaching it to more people!

It's free!

The Fala Suji language is completely free. Documentation is completely free. There is no copyright on this language. Use it, modify it however you like!

Poems in Fala Suji

penoja muto (People)

Penoja muto ike pode i tado a bono; kajo time ma ike kono a me muto, me muto ike pode i tado nene.

People can be good. But when you know them, they may not be.

kene (The war)

Vida ike tado a kene. Ike kene a kono muto. Ma iko jo a vere.

Life is a war. Fight wisely. You will win everything.

vida Inidiha (Indian life)

Luki ike tado a luki muto. Muno ike tado a dita. Vida ike tado a peko. Penoja bono ike kuja.

The sun is bright. The moon is crazy. The life is short. The good person helps.
