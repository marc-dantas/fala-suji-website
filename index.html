Fala Suji - The sweet language

What is Fala Suji?

Fala Suji is a conlang that aims to improve communication with fewer words than common languages. It's based on Toki Pona, by Sonja Lang

How to speak complex words with <200 word vocabulary?

There's a type of word called modifier. These words work as adjectives or adverbs to modify the sense of words.

To say a more complex word. You need to "disassemble" the word to translate it.

Learning the language

In this website, there's a complete documentation of Fala Suji, so you can read it and join our Discord community and learn much more!

Contribute!
